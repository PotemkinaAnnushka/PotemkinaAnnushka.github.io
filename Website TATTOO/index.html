OUR SERVICES

COSMETIC TATTOO

Thanks to my great experience and artistic skill, I will make any image on your body. You can choose a drawing from catalogs in the studio, bring your own image or order an individual sketch, which will combine my skills and all your wishes

PERMANENNT MAKEUP

Permanent makeup is a cosmetic technique that implies tattoos, resulting in a permanent pigmentation of the dermis; the purpose is to create designs that resemble traditional makeup, such as eye lining or lip eyeliner and other permanent enhancing colors to the skin of the face

TRIHOPIGMENTATION

This is an innovative and safe method that helps to hide baldness and hair thinning, various scars and scars on the scalp. This procedure is much cheaper than hair transplantation, the effect of it lasts up to 10 years

PIERCING

Our experts do piercing of various parts of the body. They will listen to your wishes, if necessary, will advise. The main thing is that everything will be done quickly and painlessly, at the highest level. In our studio you will get exactly what you came for - beauty, style and individuality

SCAR CAMOUFLAGE

This non-invasive, paramedical tattoo procedure can be performed on any part of the body for any skin type and tone. Camouflage of defects involves the introduction of a pigment matched to the color of the surrounding healthy skin. This allows you to hide scars, scars and stretch marks

MANICURE

For my beautiful clients, I am pleased to offer a gel manicure service. All instruments for the procedure have undergone a full cycle of disinfection. I have a large palette polish colors (over 400) and a wide variety of designs
